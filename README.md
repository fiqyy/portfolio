Fiqy’s Portfolio is a modern, responsive web portfolio designed to showcase Youssef Fiqy’s skills, personality, and projects in a clean single-page experience. It highlights his role as a web developer with a strong hero section and clear navigation that guides visitors through who he is, what he builds, and how to contact him.

Overview
Single-page layout with smooth navigation between Home, About, Projects, and Contact.

Prominent hero section introducing “Hi, I'm Youssef Fiqy” and “I'm a Web Developer” to immediately communicate identity and role.

Clean, readable typography and simple color choices that keep the focus on content and projects.

Key features
Hero Section: Central heading and subheading presenting Youssef’s name and web developer title, creating a strong first impression for visitors.

Navbar Navigation: Top navigation links that let users jump quickly to the About, Projects, and Contact sections without losing context.

About Section: Space dedicated to describing Youssef’s background, skills, and interests as a web developer, helping visitors understand his profile.

Projects Section: A dedicated area to list and describe selected projects, allowing potential clients or recruiters to see practical work examples.

Contact Section: A clear way for visitors to reach out, encouraging collaboration, job opportunities, or general inquiries.

Technical style
Responsive layout that adapts to different screen sizes, ensuring a good experience on desktop and mobile devices.

Simple, structured HTML and styling that focus on clarity and usability, making the portfolio easy to read and navigate.
